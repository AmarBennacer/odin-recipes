My Favorite Pasta

Description

Creating a delightful pasta dish is a flavorful journey that starts with boiling al dente pasta of your choice, be it spaghetti, penne, or linguine. While the pasta cooks, prepare a luscious sauce by sautéing garlic and onions in olive oil until fragrant, then adding crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, and a pinch of red pepper flakes for a delightful kick. Allow the sauce to simmer, letting the aromas meld together beautifully. Once the pasta is ready, drain it and toss into the sauce, ensuring every strand is coated in its richness. For the finishing touch, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top, and if you desire, garnish with fresh basil leaves. The result is a mouthwatering pasta dish that tantalizes the taste buds with its harmonious blend of flavors, perfect for a satisfying meal any day of the week.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne, or linguine)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • A pinch of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onions, sautéing until they become fragrant and slightly golden.
  3. Lower the heat and carefully add the crushed tomatoes to the skillet. Stir in the dried basil, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using). Season with salt and pepper to taste. Let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce, tossing it to coat evenly with the luscious tomato mixture.
  5. Serve the pasta in individual dishes or a large serving bowl. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top, and garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  6. Enjoy your mouthwatering pasta dish that bursts with harmonious flavors and satisfies your cravings!
